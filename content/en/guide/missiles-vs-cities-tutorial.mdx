<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step City Progression Guide">

Building a massive metropolis requires a deliberate strategy. Randomly placing structures will leave your base vulnerable to high-damage missile strikes from powerful players. Follow these steps to build an optimized, resilient city:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Establish Your Economic Foundation">
    Begin by building basic residential and commercial zones. These structures generate a passive stream of cash over time, allowing you to fund your early missile upgrades without relying solely on successful attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Deploy Air Defense Interceptors">
    Before launching your first offensive strike, build at least two basic air defense turrets. Position them on the outer perimeter of your city to intercept incoming enemy rockets before they reach your core high-value buildings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Unlock Tier-1 Missile Launchers">
    Equip your first set of basic missiles. These early-game rockets have lower damage profiles but feature fast reload times, making them excellent for testing enemy defenses and farming weaker, undefended cities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Upgrade Core City Infrastructure">
    As your cash reserves grow, upgrade your existing buildings to increase their structural integrity (HP) and passive cash yield. Upgraded buildings provide a much stronger shield against hostile bombardments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Warning: Avoid Early Aggression">
Do not attack highly ranked players during your first ten minutes of gameplay. Attacking strong players will draw their attention, and their high-tier missiles can easily bypass basic air defenses.
</Callout>

</Section>
