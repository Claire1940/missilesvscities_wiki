<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Strategies & Attack Tips">

Launching missiles randomly will waste valuable resources and leave you open to counterattacks. To become a top-tier player, apply these tactical combat strategies:

- **Focus Fire**: Concentrate your shots on a single target building. Partially damaged buildings do not yield maximum cash rewards.
- **Watch the Cool-downs**: Time your launches so you always have a defensive or offensive volley ready. Spreading out your shots ensures a constant stream of pressure.
- **Server Selection**: If a server is filled with hostile, high-level players targeting your base, do not hesitate to hop to a quieter server to farm peacefully.
- **Rebuild Quickly**: If your city is struck, repair your structures immediately to restore your passive income generation.
