### S-Tier: Heavy Thermobaric Missiles
These high-impact warheads are designed for absolute destruction. They feature a massive blast radius capable of damaging multiple adjacent structures in a single strike. Prioritize upgrading these missiles as they yield the highest cash returns per successful launch.

### A-Tier: Tactical Cruise Missiles
Featuring superior guidance systems, Cruise Missiles excel at targeting specific, high-value buildings within a rival city. They bypass basic structural shielding and provide reliable, consistent damage for steady farming.

### B-Tier: Medium Ballistic Missiles
The workhorse of the mid-game. These missiles offer a balanced mix of moderate damage and reasonable reload times. They are perfect for grinding out cash when transitioning from a small settlement to a fortified metropolis.

### C-Tier: Starter Rockets
Your default weapon system. While cheap and fast to reload, they lack the damage required to quickly bring down upgraded enemy walls. Replace these as soon as you unlock Tier B options.
