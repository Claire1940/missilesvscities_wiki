<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Cash Farming Strategy">

To maintain a top-tier defense, you need a constant influx of cash. Farming in Missiles vs Cities is a rhythmic process that involves balancing your attack cycles with your upgrade windows. Follow these steps to maximize your hourly earnings.

  <Step num="1" title="Initialize Base Growth">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Optimal Loadout">

  <Step num="3" title="Identify High-Value Targets">
    Scan the map for enemy buildings. Focus on structures that appear upgraded, as these typically yield higher cash rewards upon destruction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Attack Loop">

  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Immediate Reinvestment">

  </Step>
</Steps>
